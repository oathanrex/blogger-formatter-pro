## ğŸš€ How to Use

### 1. Fill Post Details
Enter your **Post Title**, **Post Summary**, and **Image URL** in the top fields. This information is used for Schema generation.

### 2. Enter Focus Keyword
Type your main target keyword in the **Focus Keyword** box. The sidebar will show you how many times it appears and calculate its density.

### 3. Write Your Content
Use simple Markdown-style formatting:
- **Headings:** Use `##` before a line (e.g., `## My Heading`).
- **Lists:** Use `-` or `1.` for bullet points or numbered lists.
- **FAQs:** Simply type `Q: Your Question` and `A: Your Answer` on separate lines.
- **Links:** Just paste your link (e.g., `https://amazon.in/deal`), and it will be converted automatically.

### 4. Copy & Publish
- Click the **"Copy All-in-One HTML"** button.
- Go to your **Blogger Dashboard** > **New Post**.
- Switch to **HTML View** (click the Pencil icon).
- Paste the code and click **Publish**.
